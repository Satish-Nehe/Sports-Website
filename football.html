Football

What is football ?
Football is a very popular sport that is played at high schools, colleges, and professional stadiums. It involves running, passing, kicking, and body contact. A football game is played between two teams of 11 players each. A team scores points by moving a ball across the other team's goal line.

Football Information

How it's played ?
Two teams of 11 players compete to score goals by moving the ball into the other team's goal. Players use their bodies to move the ball, without using their hands or arms. Where it's played Football is played at high schools, colleges, and professional stadiums. It's estimated that around 200 countries play the game, with a total of about 250 million players. How it's scored ? A team scores a point by moving the ball across the other team's goal line. Match length A football match is usually 90 minutes long, with a break in the middle called half-time. Added time may be added after half-time to compensate for time lost during the game. How matches can end ? A match can end in a tie, or it can go into extra time or a penalty shootout to determine the winner. The first World Cup The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Montevideo, Uruguay. In India The Indian senior national football team has won two gold medals at the Asian Games.

Football Rules

Football Rule 1
Offside : This rule prevents players from waiting near the opponent's goal for a pass, which is considered unsportsmanlik

Football Rule 2
Penality Kick : This is awarded when a defensive player fouls an attacking player or commits a handball in their team's penalty area.

Football Rule 3
Free kick : This is awarded to the opposing team when a foul, unsportsmanlike conduct, or other rule violation occurs

Football Rule 4
Ball in or out off play : The ball is in play from the start of each playing period until the end, except when it leaves the field or play is stopped by the referee.
